About Us

POP MART is a global brand known for its collectible art toys, featuring unique designs and iconic characters loved by fans worldwide. Blending creativity, storytelling, and a fun unboxing experience, POP MART creates a playful and engaging collectible culture.

uEach collection we release is built around a distinct theme, with characters that carry their own personality and story. Through our blind box concept, every purchase becomes a moment of anticipation, where what you get is always a surprise.
