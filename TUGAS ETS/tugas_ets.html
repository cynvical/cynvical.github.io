About Us

POP MART is a global brand known for its collectible art toys, featuring unique designs and iconic characters loved by fans worldwide. Blending creativity, storytelling, and a fun unboxing experience, POP MART creates a playful and engaging collectible culture.

  • Exclusive character designs by international artists
  • Blind box concept with an element of surprise
  • A growing global community of collectors

Each collection we release is built around a distinct theme, with characters that carry their own personality and story. Through our blind box concept, every purchase becomes a moment of anticipation, where what you get is always a surprise.

Crafting an unforgettable experience to light up your every #POPMoment

Focusing on quality, experience, and customer satisfaction, we curate a world where art meets play, ensuring every unboxing is a moment of pure, unpredictable joy.

01 What is POP MART?

POP MART is a global collectible brand that creates designer toys featuring unique characters, creative storytelling, and surprise unboxing experiences through blind boxes.

02 What makes blind boxes special?

Each blind box contains a mystery figure, making every purchase exciting and unpredictable. Collectors enjoy the thrill of discovering which character they will get.

03 Why do people collect POP MART figures?

POP MART figures are designed with strong artistic identity and limited releases, making them fun to collect, display, and trade within a global community of fans.

Artists

Meet the wonderful minds behind POP MART!

Lang

Artist and Curator of Hirono

Known for creating the contemplative world of Hirono, Lang focuses on capturing the raw, often overlooked emotions of childhood.

Kasing Lung

Artist and Curator of The Monsters

A renowned illustrator and the creator of the whimsical "The Monsters" series, Kasing Lung is celebrated for his unique Mythology-inspired aesthetic.

Ayan Deng

Artist and Curator of DIMOO

Ayan Deng is the visionary behind DIMOO, the boy with the cloud-shaped hair who dreams of traveling through vast, fantastical worlds.

KaKa

Artist and Curator of Nyota

Through the character Nyota, KaKa explores the beauty of retro aesthetics and the quiet joy of everyday moments
